Our History

Numa Group was founded in 2014 by a young, passionate, and highly ambitious entrepreneur with a vision to bring about fundamental change from Africa. The company was initially incorporated in the Republic of South Africa and has since expanded to other countries, including Lesotho, Zimbabwe, and Rwanda.

Meet our Founder

Augastine Jongwe

Augustine Jongwe is a tech lover and entrepreneur at heart. With a passion for innovation and a drive to bring about fundamental change in the tech industry, he has worked in various roles in the tech space, gaining valuable experience and insights along the way.

In 2018, Augustine founded Numa Group in South Africa, with the goal of providing innovative technology solutions that empower businesses and individuals to achieve their full potential. Today, Numa Group has expanded its operations to include several countries in Africa, and Augustine remains committed to driving growth and success through technology.
